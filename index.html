Use the Left and Right arrow keys to point the turret. Use the Spacebar to fire your cannon.

  • Shoot the helicopters down for +3 score.
  • Shoot the jets down for +5 score.
  • Shoot the paratroopers down for +1 score.
  • If a bomb lands on the ground it's -5 to your score.
  • If 5 troopers land it's Game Over!
  • Shoot the jets down before they drop a bomb on your turret.
  • If a bomb hits your battery it's an instant Game Over!
  • Tip: Prioritize the paratroopers and danger close bombs!
